Did you know that importing raw materials or finished goods via a Free Trade Warehousing Zone (FTWZ) can defer customs duty indefinitely and save up to 18% in cash flow by avoiding immediate GST payment? 💸📦

For MNCs importing goods into India, FTWZ serves as a legal tax-optimization channel. Here is a direct breakdown of how importing through Genex FTWZ zones saves you operational cash:

Import Logistics Comparison

Traditional Import Model
  • 18% GST + customs duty payable immediately upon landing.
  • High port demurrage charges if clearance is delayed.
  • Re-exporting requires tedious drawback procedures.
Genex FTWZ Model Duty Deferred
  • Zero GST/Customs until final domestic warehouse dispatch.
  • De-consolidate and store up to 2 years with no interest.
  • Re-export directly with zero domestic duty friction.

Overview

Genex Logistics (legally Genex Logisolutions Private Limited) is an ISO 9001:2015 certified, technology-driven contract logistics and 3PL warehousing provider operating across India. Established in 2011, Genex was created to fill the critical gap in precision logistics for industries requiring zero-error compliance and JIT (Just-in-Time) production support.

We support manufacturing operations in the automotive, industrial engineering, electronics, consumer durables, and pharmaceutical sectors. In addition to domestic warehousing and freight management, Genex operates Free Trade Warehousing Zone (FTWZ) capabilities in key industrial corridors, allowing multinational corporations to set up duty-free consolidation hubs, defer taxes legally, and execute efficient custom clearance programs matching real-time domestic demands.
